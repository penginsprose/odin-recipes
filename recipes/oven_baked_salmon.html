Chef Pengin's Oven-Baked Salmon Recipe

Description

henlo hoomans! its chef pengin back with one of his easy-to-make recipes involving pengin's favourite ingredient, SALMON ^^

today, he brings you the recipe for the juiciest oven-baked salmon that hooman likes as well

and he's going to teach you how to make it without needing to put your hooman into the oven ehe

Ingredients

  • 4 Salmon Fillets, 6oz each
  • 2 tablespoons Olive Oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon Salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon Black Pepper
  • 2 teaspoons Minced Garlic
  • 1 teaspoon Italian herb seasoning blend
  • 1 medium lemon

Steps

  1. Preheat Hooman's Oven to 400 degrees and watch the world burn
  2. Arrange Salmon Fillets on Baking Tray and Season with Salt and Pepper
  3. Sit in Oil, Garlic, Herbs, Lemon
  4. Bake for 15 minutes until Salmon is Not Transparent
  5. Eat Salmon
  6. Receive Cuddles from Hoomans
  7. Back to Sleep!
